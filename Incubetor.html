Step 1: Install Metamask

1. Go to https://metamask.io and download for your device.

2. After downloading, set up and new wallet and save your keys.

3. Add Binance Smart Chain to your network list by clicking on the dropdown and selecting "Custom RPC".

4. Enter the config below and save.

Step 2: Get BNB in your Metamask wallet.

Step 3: Buy $DPAD (For Metamask Mobile App)

1. Open Browser in Metamask App.

  • 2. Open dpad.finance in the browser. If you have an invite link, then paste that link for referral bonus.

  • 3. Enter the BNB amount that you want to invest in the $DPAD token. Click Buy Now.

  • 4. Confirm the transaction request.

  • 5. Voila! It’s done

Step 1: Install Trust Wallet and get BNB in it.

  • 1. Go to https://trustwallet.com/ and download and install the app.

  • 2. Get Binance Smart Chain $BNB tokens

  • Step 2: Install Trust Wallet and get BNB in it.

    1. Open DApp Browser in Trust Wallet (Android)

  • 2. Searchdpad.finance in the URL bar, or paste your referral invite link

  • 4. Change the chain connection to Smart Chain
